## 1. NATURE OF ENGAGEMENT

1.1 The Contractor is engaged as a Business Development Manager.

1.2 The Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of the Company. The Contractor is solely responsible for her own ACC levies, income tax, and GST (if registered).

1.3 The Contractor is not entitled to annual leave, sick leave, KiwiSaver contributions, or other employee benefits under the Employment Relations Act 2000.

1.4 **No Agency:** The Contractor is not an agent or legal representative of YourHQ. The Contractor will not make representations, warranties, or commitments on behalf of YourHQ beyond the standard product offerings outlined in Schedule A without prior written approval.

## 2. TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 This agreement commences on 01/05/2026 and continues until terminated by either party.

2.2 Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 14 days' written notice.

2.3 **Immediate Termination:** The Company may terminate immediately without notice if the Contractor:
- Commits fraud, theft, or serious misconduct;
- Causes serious reputational damage to YourHQ;
- Is subject to bankruptcy, receivership, or liquidation proceedings;
- Fails to pay taxes or ACC levies, or is subject to an IRD or ACC investigation that could impact YourHQ; or
- Breaches confidentiality, non-compete, or intellectual property provisions of this agreement.

2.4 **Handover:** Upon termination, the Contractor shall provide a written list of all active leads and their status, transfer all client materials to YourHQ, and return any company property.

2.5 **Commission During Notice:** Sales successfully closed during the notice period remain eligible for commission. The Contractor's right to receive recurring commissions ("the tail") on Active Clients secured during the engagement continues after termination, for as long as those clients remain Active Clients.

2.6 **Notices:** Any notice under this agreement must be sent by email to the Contractor's and Company's designated email addresses listed above. Notices are deemed received 24 hours after sending, provided no delivery failure notification is received.

## 3. SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED

The Contractor shall provide the following services:

- **Lead Generation:** Engaging in Facebook groups and local networks to build brand presence.
- **Sales:** Conducting discovery calls, overcoming objections, and securing signed clients via Stripe.
- **Retention:** Conducting 14-day follow-up calls and securing Google Reviews.
- **Availability:** The Contractor agrees to respond to inbound leads within a reasonable timeframe (aiming for <4 hours on business days) to ensure conversion rates.

## 4. COMMISSION & PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 **Commission Only:** There is no base salary or hourly rate. Payment is 100% performance-based.

4.2 **Schedule of Rates:** Commissions are paid according to Schedule A (attached).

4.3 **Payment Timing:**
- **Upfront Fees:** Payable on the 20th of the month following the expiration of the Client's "Love Your Launch" refund window.
- **Power-Ups Added Post-Signup:** Payable on the 20th of the month following the month the Power-Up is purchased and paid for by the client.
- **Recurring (The Tail):** Payable monthly in arrears on the 20th of the month.

4.4 **GST:** All rates in Schedule A are exclusive of GST. If the Contractor is GST registered, she must provide a valid Tax Invoice adding 15% GST.

4.5 **Commission Disputes:** If the Contractor disputes a commission calculation, she must notify YourHQ in writing within 7 days of receiving payment. YourHQ will review Stripe records and respond within 14 days. If unresolved, Clause 10 (Dispute Resolution) applies.

4.6 **Commission Review:** Both parties agree to formally review the commission structure on the 12-month anniversary of this agreement's commencement date. The purpose of the review is to assess whether the structure is working fairly for both parties in light of actual sales volume, retention rates, and operational realities. Either party may propose adjustments. Any agreed changes must be documented in writing as a variation to this agreement under Clause 14.2. If no changes are agreed, the existing structure continues unchanged. Subsequent reviews may be scheduled by mutual agreement.

## 5. FINANCIAL PROTECTIONS

5.1 **No Refund, No Pay:** Commission is paid only on realized revenue. If a client refunds under the "Love Your Launch" guarantee (before the site goes live), no commission is payable.

5.2 **Clawback:** If a client cancels or defaults on payment, any unearned commission already paid to the Contractor may be deducted (set-off) from future commission payments.

5.3 **The "Tail" Conditions:** Recurring monthly commissions are paid at the rate specified in Schedule A for as long as the client remains an Active Client. There is no step-down or expiration date.

5.4 **Definition of Active Client:** An "Active Client" is defined as a customer who has paid the setup fee, successfully passed the "Love Your Launch" refund window without cancelling, and is current on their monthly subscription payments.

## 6. RETENTION BONUS

6.1 **Annual Retention Bonus:** If the overall client retention rate (measured as the percentage of Active Clients at the start of the 12-month period who remain Active Clients at the end) exceeds 85%, the Contractor shall receive a one-time bonus of $2,000 NZD.

6.2 **Measurement Period:** The retention rate is calculated annually on the anniversary of this agreement's commencement date.

6.3 **Payment Timing:** The retention bonus, if earned, is payable within 30 days of the measurement date.

6.4 **Purpose:** This bonus recognises that the Contractor's role in acquiring quality clients directly impacts long-term retention, and rewards the Contractor for contributing to sustainable business growth.

## 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 **Data Ownership:** All client data (names, emails, business details) acquired by the Contractor during this engagement is the sole property of YourHQ.

7.2 **Non-Diversion:** The Contractor must not divert YourHQ clients to other service providers.

7.3 **Confidentiality:** The Contractor shall keep confidential all information relating to YourHQ's business, including but not limited to: pricing models, commission structures, client lists, business plans, and internal processes. This obligation survives the termination of this agreement.

## 8. RESTRAINT OF TRADE

# SCHEDULE A — COMMISSION RATES

## 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

YourHQ's offering consists of:

- **Setup Fee:** $1,800 NZD + GST (one-time, fixed)
- **Caretaker Subscription (monthly, choose one):**
  - Sorted — $129/mo + GST
  - Connected — $179/mo + GST
  - Building — $249/mo + GST
- **Concierge Bolt-On (optional):** $199/mo + GST
- **Power-Ups (optional, priced individually):** e.g. booking platforms, email tools, Stripe integration, and any future add-ons offered by YourHQ.

## 2. COMMISSION STRUCTURE SUMMARY

The Contractor earns three commission streams:

- A flat upfront fee for each successful build sale.
- A recurring monthly commission ("the tail") on each active client's monthly Caretaker subscription, for as long as the client remains active.
- A flat percentage on any Power-Ups purchased by the client, whether at signup or at any later date during the client's lifecycle.

All rates are flat. They do not change based on cumulative sales volume.

The commission structure is subject to formal review on the 12-month anniversary of this agreement's commencement (see Clause 4.6 in the body of this agreement).

## 3. UPFRONT COMMISSION

Upfront commission is a flat fee paid per successful build sale, once per client after the "Love Your Launch" refund window expires.

| | Rate |
|---|---|
| **Per build** | **$300 NZD** (excl. GST) |

This rate applies regardless of any Power-Ups purchased at signup. Power-Up commissions are calculated separately under Section 4.

## 4. POWER-UP COMMISSION

The Contractor earns commission on Power-Ups purchased by any client she has secured, at any point during the client's lifecycle (at signup or later), for as long as the client remains an Active Client.

### 4.1 Power-Up Setup Fees (One-Time)

A flat **20% commission** of the Power-Up's setup price (excl. GST), paid once per Power-Up purchase. Paid on the 20th of the month following purchase.

**Worked examples:**
- Bookings Embed ($100): 20% × $100 = **$20**
- Email Platform + Lead Magnet ($250): 20% × $250 = **$50**
- Stripe Services or Digital Products ($350): 20% × $350 = **$70**
- Stripe Course Delivery ($600): 20% × $600 = **$120**
- Content Autopilot setup ($200): 20% × $200 = **$40**

### 4.2 Power-Up Recurring Fees (Monthly)

For Power-Ups that include an ongoing monthly fee (e.g. Content Autopilot at $79–99/mo), the Contractor earns **8% of the monthly Power-Up fee**, paid monthly for as long as the client remains an Active Client and continues to pay for the Power-Up.

**Worked example:** Content Autopilot at $79/mo: 8% × $79 = **$6.32/mo**

### 4.3 Eligibility

The Contractor's right to Power-Up commissions applies to all Power-Ups purchased by clients she originally secured, regardless of when the Power-Up is added. Commission ceases if the client cancels the Power-Up or terminates their subscription.

## 5. RECURRING COMMISSION ("THE TAIL")

Recurring commission is paid monthly as a flat **8% of the client's monthly Caretaker subscription + Concierge bolt-on (if applicable)**, excl. GST. Paid for as long as the client remains an Active Client.

| Plan | Monthly tail to Contractor |
|---|---|
| Sorted ($129/mo) | **$10.32/mo** |
| Connected ($179/mo) | **$14.32/mo** |
| Building ($249/mo) | **$19.92/mo** |
| + Concierge bolt-on ($199/mo) | adds **$15.92/mo** |

**Worked example:** Connected client with Concierge added.
- Monthly tail: 8% × ($179 + $199) = 8% × $378 = **$30.24/mo**
- Continues at this rate for as long as the client remains active.

## 6. TAIL LONGEVITY

6.1 The Contractor's recurring tail on a client continues at the full 8% rate for as long as that client remains an Active Client. There is no step-down or expiration date.

6.2 The tail ceases when the client cancels or terminates their subscription, subject to the pause and clawback provisions in Clauses 5.1, 5.2, and Section 7 of this Schedule.

6.3 **Rationale:** This structure rewards the Contractor for bringing in clients who stay long-term and creates clean alignment between sales quality and business sustainability.

6.4 **GST:** All rates in Schedule A are exclusive of GST. Commissions are calculated on the **Net Revenue (excl. GST)** received from the client. No commission is payable on the GST portion of any sale.

## 7. SPECIAL CONDITIONS

- **Caretaker Tier Changes (Up or Down):** If a client moves between Caretaker tiers (e.g. Sorted → Connected, or Building → Connected), the Contractor's recurring commission adjusts to the new tier rate immediately. No additional upfront commission is paid for tier upgrades.
- **Concierge Add/Remove:** If a client adds or removes the Concierge bolt-on, the Contractor's recurring commission adjusts immediately to reflect the new monthly amount.
- **Cancellations:** If a client cancels their subscription, recurring commission stops immediately.
- **Pauses:** If a client pauses their subscription (if such option is offered), recurring commission is paused for the same period.
- **Refunds on Power-Ups:** If a Power-Up is refunded, any commission paid on that Power-Up is subject to clawback under Clause 5.2.

## 8. ANNUAL RETENTION BONUS

- **Threshold:** 85% or higher client retention over the 12-month measurement period.
- **Bonus Amount:** $2,000 NZD (one-time, annual)
- **Calculation:** Retention % = (Active Clients at End of Period / Active Clients at Start of Period) × 100. Clients acquired during the period are excluded from this calculation.

## 9. EXAMPLE EARNINGS CALCULATIONS

**Example 1: Single Connected Client (no Power-Ups, no Concierge)**
- Upfront: $300
- Tail (24 months): $14.32 × 24 = $343.68
- **Total over 24 months: $643.68**
- Tail continues at $14.32/mo for as long as client remains active.

**Example 2: Single Building Client with Concierge (no Power-Ups)**
- Upfront: $300
- Tail (24 months): 8% × ($249 + $199) × 24 = $35.84 × 24 = $860.16
- **Total over 24 months: $1,160.16**
- Tail continues at $35.84/mo for as long as client remains active.

**Example 3: Connected Client adds Stripe Services Power-Up at month 6**
- Upfront (build): $300
- Stripe Power-Up setup commission: 20% × $350 = $70
- Tail on $179/mo (24 months): $14.32 × 24 = $343.68
- **Total over 24 months: $713.68**

**Example 4: At 100 Active Clients (40% Sorted, 50% Connected, 10% Building, no Concierge)**
- Sorted: 40 × $10.32 = $412.80
- Connected: 50 × $14.32 = $716.00
- Building: 10 × $19.92 = $199.20
- **Monthly Tail: ~$1,328/month**
- **Annual Tail: ~$15,936/year**

**Example 5: At 275 Active Clients (Financial Freedom Target)**
Assuming 40% Sorted, 50% Connected, 10% Building:
- Sorted: 110 × $10.32 = $1,135.20
- Connected: 137.5 × $14.32 = $1,969.00
- Building: 27.5 × $19.92 = $547.80
- **Monthly Tail: ~$3,652/month**
- **Annual Tail: ~$43,824/year**

**Example 6: At 650 Active Clients (Scale Achievement)**
Assuming 40% Sorted, 50% Connected, 10% Building:
- Sorted: 260 × $10.32 = $2,683.20

*Note: Examples 4–6 exclude Concierge bolt-on uptake and Power-Up commissions, both of which would add to the totals.*
