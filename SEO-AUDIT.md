## AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Recommendations

### What is AEO?
AEO optimizes for AI-powered answer engines like:
- ChatGPT Search
- Perplexity
- Google AI Overviews
- Claude (that's me!)

### Key AEO Strategies:
1. **Structured Data** - Makes your content machine-readable
2. **FAQ Schema** - Helps AI extract Q&A pairs
3. **Clear, Concise Answers** - Direct responses to common questions
4. **Semantic HTML** - Proper heading hierarchy
5. **Rich Snippets** - Enhanced search results
